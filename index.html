ABOUT

We are here to keep track of your medical history!

Why?

The problem was discovered by evaluating our personal medical needs. As we are a young group, we have just started to find out how it is like to be a responsible adult. From the personal medical state point of view, we were raised with the cult of keeping in the very back of a drawer all the health related papers. Elder people, who are more conservative do like this approach. However, in the technology era, it is more efficient to have access to a powerful tool that keeps track of your medical history.

What?

Taking into account the ones mentioned above, we try to come in hand to people and companies that want to raise their medical test experiences to a new level. Therefore, we implement a smart application, which can be seen as a rich database in which our clients can input and find their medical status at any time. Moreover, this can be a huge improvement for medical centers which care about their patients and desire to offer them the best experience, as well as companies whose activity depends on medical statistics and want to easily access a medical database, in legal methods, taking into account the privacy terms and conditions.

Competition

Currently, there are no apps that allow data uploading. However, medical centers use their own databases to store and offer to patients their medical results. For instance, Synevo and Medlife offer codes as a key to their database, providing a specific result, but multiple tests conducted within different companies would lead to many codes and different platforms that the results can be accessed from.
